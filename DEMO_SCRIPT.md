# Customer Demo Script

## 1. Start with the business problem

“Many customers ask the same questions: what services do you provide, what steel materials are available, what details are needed for CNC cutting, and how can they send a drawing or photo. This website answers those questions and guides customers to request a quotation.”

## 2. Show the homepage

Point out:

- The official CSK logo is visible in the header and hero section.
- The design uses navy and gold for a premium steel-company look.
- Main services are clear: CNC sheet cutting, steel materials, gate/fence hardware, and custom fabrication.
- Customers have clear actions: `Request a Quote` and `View Services`.

## 3. Open Services page

Explain:

- Each service has a short explanation.
- Customers can understand what CSK does before calling.
- The page tells customers what information to prepare: material, thickness, size, quantity, drawing, photo, or sample.

## 4. Open Products page

Explain:

- This page organizes steel product categories.
- Later, real photos, sizes, thickness, and stock information can be added.
- This makes the website useful as a simple product catalog.

## 5. Open Projects page

Explain:

- This section is for real CSK project photos.
- Photos of CNC work, gate/fence work, material stock, and custom fabrication will build customer trust.

## 6. Open Quote page

Use this sample data:

```text
Name: Sokha
Phone/Telegram: 012 345 678
Service: CNC sheet cutting
Material: 2mm steel sheet
Size/Quantity: 1200x2400mm, 10 pieces
Details: Decorative panel for gate
```

Click `Create Telegram Message`.

Say:

“The website creates a clean quotation message. In production, this can be sent automatically to Telegram, Google Sheets, email, or the company backend system.”

## 7. Open Contact page

Explain:

- Customers can call directly or message on Telegram.
- The map area can be replaced with the real CSK shop location.

## 8. Close with next steps

“For launch, we only need real photos, exact address, confirmed contact information, and form integration. The Next.js structure and brand style are already ready.”
